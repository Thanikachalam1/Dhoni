Mahendra Singh Dhoni, commonly known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian international cricketer and captain. He is considered one of the most successful captains in cricket history. Born on July 7,1981, Dhoni made his debut for the Indian cricket team in 2004. He is known for his exceptional skills as a wicketkeeper-batsman and is regarded as one of the best finishers in limited-overs cricket

timeline

1981: Mahendra Singh Dhoni is born in Ranchi, Bihar (now in Jharkhand), India.

2004: Dhoni makes his international debut for the Indian cricket team in an ODI match against Bangladesh.

2005: He scores his first international century against Pakistan in an ODI match.

2007: Dhoni becomes the captain of the Indian cricket team for the ICC World Twenty20 tournament.

2011: He leads India to victory in the ICC Cricket World Cup.

2014: Dhoni announces his retirement from Test cricket.

2016: He leads India to victory in the ICC World Twenty20 tournament held in India.

2020: Dhoni announces his retirement from international cricket.
